I am an oceanographer in the Department of Earth Sciences at the University of Oxford.
I recently submitted my PhD thesis, titled Lagrangian Perspectives on Ocean Ventilation.
I have primarily worked on ocean ventilation, as well as investigating the role of the ocean in the uptake of atmospheric CO2.
